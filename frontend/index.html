📄 Documentation & About

About Gantabya Sahayak

An open WebGIS platform built to modernise Nepal's tourism infrastructure through spatial data and real-time intelligence.

🎯
Our Mission

Gantabya Sahayak (गन्तब्य सहायक) was created to be Nepal's definitive digital travel companion — combining government spatial datasets, real-time road conditions, OpenStreetMap data, and weather APIs into one unified WebGIS platform.

We empower tourists, trekkers, and researchers with location-aware intelligence: know which roads are passable after monsoon, which trails are open, where the nearest hospital is when you're deep in the Himalayas, and which month is best for Rara Lake.

The platform is built entirely on open-source technology — MapLibre GL JS for cartography, FastAPI for the backend, PostGIS for spatial queries, and GeoJSON for interoperable data exchange.

Data Sources
Our spatial datasets are derived from authoritative government and open-data sources, processed and harmonised for the WebGIS platform.
  • ▸ Survey Department of Nepal — Admin Boundaries
  • ▸ Department of Roads — Road Network GIS
  • ▸ TAAN — Trekking Routes & Permits
  • ▸ OpenStreetMap Nepal — POIs & Emergency
  • ▸ Open-Meteo — Live Weather API
  • ▸ UNESCO — World Heritage Site Registry
🚀
Getting Started
  1. Sign in with your Google account using the button below or in the navigation.
  2. Select a tool from the dashboard — nearest place, trek analysis, buffer, emergency, or trip planning.
  3. Allow location to enable GPS-based features like nearest emergency services.
  4. Use filters & layers in each module's sidebar to customise the map view.
  5. Trip Planner — enter budget, days, and interests to get a full itinerary.
